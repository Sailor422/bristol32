
BRISTOL32 EXTERNAL SENSOR NODE SCHEMATIC
=========================================

POWER SUPPLY SECTION:
---------------------
12V_MARINE_BATT (+) --> VOLTAGE_REGULATOR --> 3.3V_VCC (+)
                      |
                      +--> GND (-)

ESP32 HELTEC WIFI LORA 32 V2:
-----------------------------
PIN CONNECTIONS:
- GPIO13 --> MMWAVE_SENSOR (motion detection)
- GPIO21 --> I2C_SDA (BME280, SSD1306)
- GPIO22 --> I2C_SCL (BME280, SSD1306)
- VCC --> 3.3V_VCC
- GND --> GND

SX1276 LORA MODULE:
-------------------
PIN CONNECTIONS:
- NSS --> ESP32_SPI_CS
- MOSI --> ESP32_SPI_MOSI
- MISO --> ESP32_SPI_MISO
- SCK --> ESP32_SPI_SCK
- RST --> ESP32_GPIO_RST
- DIO0 --> ESP32_GPIO_DIO0
- ANTENNA --> 433MHz_ANTENNA
- VCC --> 3.3V_VCC
- GND --> GND

MMWAVE MOTION SENSOR:
---------------------
PIN CONNECTIONS:
- OUT --> ESP32_GPIO13
- VCC --> 3.3V_VCC
- GND --> GND

BME280 ENVIRONMENTAL SENSOR:
---------------------------
PIN CONNECTIONS:
- SDA --> ESP32_GPIO21 (I2C)
- SCL --> ESP32_GPIO22 (I2C)
- VCC --> 3.3V_VCC
- GND --> GND

SSD1306 OLED DISPLAY:
---------------------
PIN CONNECTIONS:
- SDA --> ESP32_GPIO21 (I2C)
- SCL --> ESP32_GPIO22 (I2C)
- VCC --> 3.3V_VCC
- GND --> GND

I2C PULL-UP RESISTORS:
----------------------
- 4.7kΩ between SDA (GPIO21) and 3.3V_VCC
- 4.7kΩ between SCL (GPIO22) and 3.3V_VCC

ANTENNA:
--------
- 433MHz whip antenna connected to SX1276 ANTENNA pin
- Optional: Matching network (LC filter)

POWER CONSUMPTION ESTIMATES:
----------------------------
- ESP32: 80-240mA (depending on WiFi/LoRa usage)
- SX1276: 20-120mA (TX/RX modes)
- BME280: <1mA
- SSD1306: 20mA (with backlight)
- mmWave: 50-100mA
- TOTAL: ~200-500mA peak

DESIGN NOTES:
-------------
- All I2C devices share the same bus (GPIO21/22)
- SPI bus used for LoRa communication
- GPIO13 used for motion detection interrupt
- 12V marine power requires proper voltage regulation
- Consider ESD protection on antenna and I/O lines
